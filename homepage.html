About Me

Hi, I'm Victoria and I've been passionate about caring for animals since I was a kid. It has always been my dream to make a living playing with and caring for animals. I have 5 years experience working and managing a doggie daycare in Old Hickory. Unfortunately, due to the coronavirus pandemic, we were forced to close down. It is my dream to continue my passion with animals.
